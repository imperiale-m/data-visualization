Tree Carbon Storage impact on Total Saving in Trento

Sankey diagram that shows the impact of Carbon storage on Total saving of the top 10 abundance tree in Trento.

Sankey diagram

A Sankey chart is a type of representation that depicts the movement of data from one set to another. It's useful if we want to have a high-level view of how data flow and change from one stage to the next.

Final considerations

- The data is normalized (percentage) and the links are sorted in an attempt to make the graph more clear (the real value is visible on the tooltip).
- The value of carbon_storage looks wrong compared to what I would expect by doing a little bit of research.
- The target of the second level is total_annual_benefits because this seemed to me the one closest to the request (but there was also a carbon_storage_eur column).
- Neither carbon_storage nor carbon_storage_eur were used in the original project.
- Assuming the data is correct we can notice that Celtis Australis has a higher contribution to total saving despite a low carbon storage.
